Addressing Challenges

Many places have started addressing these challenges by improving road infrastructure near mountainous regions and making these areas more urban. Building better roads helps ensure safer and more efficient transportation of food and supplies, even in difficult terrain. Additionally, enhancing urbanization in these regions supports economic development, providing better access to essential services and resources. Innovative solutions, such as using helicopters for quick and direct delivery in emergencies, have also been implemented to overcome the barriers posed by harsh weather and rugged landscapes. These efforts collectively help ensure that food and other crucial supplies reach remote communities reliably and promptly.
