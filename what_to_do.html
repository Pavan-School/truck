What futher action needs to be taken

Further actions need to be taken to enhance transportation infrastructure, such as building more sturdy roads and bridges in mountainous regions. Investing in technology and resources for air transportation, including helicopters and drones, which can ensure timely delivery of food and supplies to those regions. Additionally, implementing sustainable agricultural practices in mountainous regions and improving storage facilities can help mitigate the impact of harsh weather conditions and economic hardships on food security in these areas.
